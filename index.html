TREND REU

Training and Research Experiences in Nonlinear Dynamics

TREND is a 10-week undergrad summer research program in nonlinear dynamics at UMD College Park, funded by the NSF (award PHY-2150399.)

Projects are computational, experimental, theoretical, or a combination of these. Topics include:

2D materials | biophysics | chaos | lasers | mathematical biology | quantum nanodevices | neural networks | optoelectronics | particle physics/particle rings | photonics | plasma reconnection | robotics | turbulence

TREND 2023 will run May 30 to August 4. Applications will open around October 2022 with a deadline of February 10, 2023.

Program Basics

Offerings

$6000 stipend, on-campus housing, travel funding, mentored research, professional development, and community-building. See details.

Eligibility

U.S. citizenship, undergrad standing, and recommended 3.3 GPA. See details.

Application

Information form, personal essay, and at least 1 letter of recommendation. See details.
