TREND REU

Training and Research Experiences in Nonlinear Dynamics

TREND is a 10-week undergrad summer research program in nonlinear dynamics at UMD College Park, funded by the NSF (award PHY1756179).

Projects are computational, experimental, theoretical, or a combination of these.

Topics include: 🦁 2D materials | biophysics | chaos | lasers | mathematical biology | nanodevices | neural networks | optoelectronics | particle physics/particle rings | plasma reconnection | robotics | turbulence

TREND 2023 will occur between May 30 and August 4. Applications will open around October 2022 with a deadline of February 10, 2023.

Program Basics

Offerings

TREND offers a $6000 stipend, housing at UMD College Park during summer, travel support, and a range of professional development and community-building activities. See details.

Eligibility

(1) U.S. citizens or permanent residents. (2) Enrolled in a college or university with anticipated graduation of December 2023 or later. (3) Minimum recommended GPA of 3.3 out of 4.0. Applicants without the recommended GPA can provide details in the application form if desired.

Application

The application includes an info form, a personal essay, and at least 1 letter of recommendation. See details.

Important Dates
